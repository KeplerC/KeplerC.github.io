Bio

I am Kaiyuan Chen, a forth-year undergraduate student from University of California, Los Angeles(UCLA). Now I am working in Wireless Networking Group (WiNG) under supervision of Songwu Lu.

My general interest is very broad. I study anything related to machine learning, artificial intelligence and big data, especially for anomaly detection and compressed sensing. I am also interested in computer network and bioinformatics.
