Bio

I am Kaiyuan Chen, a first year Ph.D. student in Computer Science from University of California, Berkeley. My current advisor is Professor John Kubiatowicz. Previously, I worked in Wireless Networking Group (WiNG) with Professor Songwu Lu.

My general interest is in computer networks, mobile and ubiquitous computing, cloud computing and data-intensive system design.
